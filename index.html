Created for you!

The majority of people who come to exercise in a gym do not use a machine in the correct way. Other (new) athletes do not dare to ask questions to employees about the use of the machine and/or the exercise itself. This is to help you!

Easy to learn videos

EASYGYM lets you watch different exercises in a fun and easy way through the homemade videos with instructions. It's as easy as scanning a qr code and watching the video. If that doesn't help watch the bottom instructions under each video!

Phone scanning

Everything is linked with QR codes! Take your smartphone out of your pocket and scan one of our QR codes at a gym exercise and it will automatically send you a to a EASYGYM page that shows a video with instructions. Just scan me!

Website and look

Do you prefer to look at exercises before or after you head to the gym? With the newly created webiste of EASYGYM it is as easy as choosing what workout you would like to learn and watch te video. Simpel and easy for you!
